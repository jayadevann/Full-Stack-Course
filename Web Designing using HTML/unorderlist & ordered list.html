french fries recepie

ingredients

  • potatoes
  • oil
  • cold water
  • rosemary
  • salt
  • red chilli powder
  1. Slice the potatoes 1/2 inch thick. ...
  2. Soak them cold in water for at least an hour or overnight....
  3. Rinse them twice with cold water and pat the completely dry.
  4. Heat oil to 300 degrees. ...
  5. Increase heat to 400 degrees. ...
  6. Place them on paper towels and sprinkle immediately with salt.
